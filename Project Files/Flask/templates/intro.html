🩺 Welcome to Liver Cirrhosis Risk Checker

Liver Cirrhosis is a progressive, chronic disease that replaces healthy liver tissue with scar tissue. It can lead to serious complications and even liver failure. Early detection is critical.

Causes

  • 🍺 Long-term alcohol abuse
  • 🦠 Chronic hepatitis B or C
  • 🍔 Non-alcoholic fatty liver disease
  • 🧬 Genetic liver disorders

Prevention

  • ❌ Limit alcohol consumption
  • 💉 Vaccinate against hepatitis
  • 🥗 Eat a liver-friendly diet
  • 🏃‍♂ Maintain a healthy weight

Complications

  • ⚠ Liver failure
  • 💉 Internal bleeding
  • 🧫 Infections
  • 🧪 Liver cancer

Why Predict Early?

  • ⏱ Early treatment improves outcomes
  • 🧠 Better quality of life
  • 💊 Prevent irreversible damage
  • 💰 Reduce long-term healthcare costs
